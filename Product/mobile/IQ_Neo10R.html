Quick Overview

The iQOO Neo 10R 5G is a performance-focused smartphone that delivers smooth gaming, fast multitasking, and solid battery life. Powered by the Snapdragon 8+ Gen 1 and featuring a 120Hz AMOLED display, it’s ideal for gamers and power users on a budget.

Performance Review

  • Gaming: Perfect for heavy gaming like BGMI, COD Mobile, and Asphalt 9. Smooth, lag-free performance with high graphics settings.
  • Display & Sound: Crisp 6.78" AMOLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate. Single speaker—loud enough but no stereo sound.
  • Camera: 50MP main camera is great in daylight. Decent for casual shots and social media, but night photography is average.
  • Battery: 5000mAh battery lasts all day. Blazing fast 120W charging gets you from 0 to 100% in around 25 minutes!
